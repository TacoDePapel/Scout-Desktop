SCOUT — MAC TEST DRIVE
======================

1. Double-click "Install Scout.command".

   If your Mac blocks it ("unidentified developer"):
   right-click the file, choose "Open", then click "Open" again.

   Still blocked? (newest Macs): Apple menu > System Settings >
   Privacy & Security > scroll down > click "Open Anyway".

2. That's it. Scout installs itself into Applications and opens.

3. The first time you record, your Mac will ask for permissions
   (Accessibility, Screen Recording). Allow them — and after turning
   on Screen Recording, quit Scout and open it again (macOS rule).

Questions or something breaks? Tell us what you clicked and what the
screen said: https://github.com/TacoDePapel/Scout-Desktop/issues
